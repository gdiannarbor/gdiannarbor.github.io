Process Changes

2015 was great!

2016 will be better!

Issues...

  • We want to give you more autonomy
  • We want to make sure you feel confident and invested
  • With so many team members, we need to also be better organized
  • Reporting, keeping tabs, tracking progress, staying on point... has been difficult and felt impersonal.
  • It's too much for me to oversee all events.

SO... Changes.

  • Each event will have an assigned Chapter Lead
  • The Chapter Lead will be who you report to, coordinate with, and get help from.
  • Each Core Chapter Leader will be assigned to 1-2 events per month (one of which will likely be a multipart workshop).
  • Each event will have (an) assigned Attending Lead(s) who attends the event, assists with setup, brings supplies and swag, gives GDI introduction, etc.
  • The assigned Chapter Lead and Attending Lead(s) do not need to be the same.

Soooo....

The assigned Chapter Lead should help get you started, work with you through the pre-planning steps, regularly check in with you to see that things are going smoothly and help guide you along the way.

Chapter Leads will report progress to Core Team.

No more Monday Slack checkins.

Organizer Meetup every 2-3 months (?)
to assign events, refine processes, socialize.

Ultimately...

This will afford you more autonomy and increase participation in the event planning and prep process.

Instead of working distantly with all of us, you'll be working closely with one of us.

Pairing & Knowledge Sharing FTW!!
